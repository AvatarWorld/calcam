Citing this project in publications:

Calcam has a DOI issued though Zenodo. 

The following citation will always refer to the most recent release version:

"Scott Silburn et al. (2018, November 6). Calcam. Zenodo. http://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1478554"


Specific versions can also be cited, and have individual DOIs. For example for v1.9.4:

"Scott Silburn et al. (2018, November 6). Calcam (Version 1.9.4). Zenodo. http://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1478555"


For more details including citation text and DOIs for different versions, please refer to:

https://zenodo.org/badge/latestdoi/92296352
